Who I Am

I am an independent iOS and web developer who builds complete products from scratch — design, code, backend, and App Store submission — all solo.

My work spans native SwiftUI and UIKit apps, Firebase-powered SaaS platforms, Cloudflare Workers for secure API proxies, and WordPress sites. Every product I ship is something I would use myself.

I care about honesty in what apps claim to do, clean code that can be maintained, and interfaces that feel natural from the first tap.
